SF 49ers

 

The San Francisco 49ers are the team that I have been a fan of for the longest time. It started as a child, when my mom wouldn't let my Dad take me to Raiders games. So growing up going to 49ers games I naturally became a fan. This last season was especially fun to be a fan as they made it to the Super Bowl. One of my favorite facts about being a fan of the 49ers is that Colin Kaepernick and I went to college together at the University of Nevada.
